The Always-On Enterprise

Today, the world is increasingly reliant on IT, where mobile offices are becoming commonplace, and data theft is a serious risk to business stability, the security of your business information is paramount. You need a backup and disaster recovery solution that will fulfil all your unique business requirements.
For today’s enterprises, this means that expectations for SLAs for data and application uptime can be revolutionized. No longer do businesses need to think in hours or days – they can think in minutes or seconds instead – for all applications, data, and workloads.
Providing businesses and enterprises of all sizes with the means to ensure Availability for virtual, physical, and cloud-based workloads through the following capabilities:

Availability Platform for the Hybrid Cloud

Enterprise Continuity

Recovery Service Level Objectives of less than 15 minutes for ALL applications and data; automated Disaster Recovery (DR) orchestration

Workload Mobility

Availability for workloads across any cloud or location, to maximize IT investments and increase flexibility

Compliance and Visibility

Proactive monitoring, reporting, testing and documentation to ensure business and regulatory requirements are met
